    • About us

    We are KZVolunteers, a socially conscious company that organizes voluntary events, provides financial assistance to those in need, and promotes nature and ecology. Through our voluntary events, we offer opportunities for individuals to dedicate their time and skills towards creating positive change. By raising funds, we provide support to those facing challenging circumstances and advocate for environmental conservation. Our event registration platform makes it easy for people to get involved and participate in special initiatives.

    A very generous person donated 50,000 tenge to firefighters


    There are multiple fires in Kazakhstan. Some very nice person voluntarily donated 50,000 tenge for food, water for firefighters

    "91" musical group became ambassadors for UNICEF

    91 intend to take on the problem of children's rights, bullying among children, including because of their appearance.

    Elena Rybakina donated to young tennis players

    She provided financial support to 14 young Kazakh tennis players, transferring 2.5 million tenge to each of her Wimbledon prize money. In total, it turns out that juniors received 35 million tenge.

    "Qazmed" volunteer organization collected money for Chingiz

    Qazmed's fundraising campaign for Chingiz has been a tremendous success. We have collected the necessary funds to assist Chingiz, who faces serious disease.
